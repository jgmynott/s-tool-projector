FAQ

Questions worth answering.

Is this investment advice?

No. Projections are statistical outputs, not recommendations. Every ticker shows its projection range and the model's historical MAPE on that symbol so you can judge the bands yourself.

Where does the data come from?

Financial Modeling Prep for fundamentals, yfinance for prices, FRED for macro reference data. Russell 3000 universe via iShares IWV holdings, refreshed daily.

Why kill the sentiment signal?

Because it failed. Post-2023 backtests on 2,478 symbols × 466 windows showed ≤0.43pp MAPE effect with worse hit rates. We don't ship dead tilts — even if they were once useful.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. Stripe, month-to-month, no contract. Cancel from your account portal — access continues through the end of the billing period.

How is my data handled?

Email + Stripe customer ID stored in a private database, never committed to source. The projection cache is open and shared (no PII). We don't sell or share user data.
