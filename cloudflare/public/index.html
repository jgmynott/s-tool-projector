03 The method

Three signals.
One composite score.

01 / Momentum

Where price is going

Trend strength, volatility-adjusted returns, and relative performance against the sector cohort. Time horizons from 21 to 252 trading days.

02 / Fundamentals

What the business is worth

Earnings quality, free cash flow, balance-sheet health, and valuation relative to historical and sector medians. Refreshed nightly.

03 / Risk

What can go wrong

Drawdown depth, beta to the index, realized volatility, and earnings proximity. Heavy penalties for tickers entering high-uncertainty windows.
